<h2 style="margin: 60px 0px 10px;">About me</h2>

Hello! I am a first-year Ph.D. student at [The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU)](https://www.polyu.edu.hk/), advised by [Prof. Maggie Wenjie Li](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~cswjli/). I am also fortunate to work closely with [Dr. Yi Cheng](https://yicheng98.github.io/) and [Dr. Jiashuo Wang](https://www4.comp.polyu.edu.hk/~csjwang/). Before joining PolyU, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from [Wuhan University (WHU)](https://www.whu.edu.cn/), where I was fortunate to be supervised by [Prof. Zuchao Li](https://zcli-charlie.github.io/) and [Prof. Hai Zhao](https://bcmi.sjtu.edu.cn/home/zhaohai/) from [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/). I also interned with the [AI Reasoning Group](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/opportunity/research-intern-for-artificial-intelligence-reasoning-group/) at [Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA)](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/lab/microsoft-research-asia/), where I worked with [Dr. Yeyun Gong](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/yegong/) and [Dr. Xiao Liu](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/xiaoliu2/).

My research interests broadly lie in **NLP for Social Good**. I am currently working on **memory for personalization**. Please feel free to reach out if you are interested in potential collaborations.
